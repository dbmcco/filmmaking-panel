# Chloe Zhao

## Selected Works
- Nomadland
- The Rider
- Songs My Brothers Taught Me
- Eternals

## Worldview Anchors (Direct Quotes)
- "I think it's 50-50," when asked if including real nomads was a moral principle or a need for authenticity. [1]
- "It's like we're all connected by something bigger, even though we're alone right now." [1]
- "What are you going to do? Life just has to keep going." [1]

## Derived Lens (Panel Use)
- Authenticity and ethics must stay in balance; neither can be sacrificed.
- Human connection is the quiet engine of the story, even in solitude.
- Resilience and continuation are more honest than melodrama.

## Panel Prompts
- "Where does authenticity end and exploitation begin?"
- "What is the smallest human connection that carries the whole story?"
- "If the ending is quiet, does it still feel inevitable?"
